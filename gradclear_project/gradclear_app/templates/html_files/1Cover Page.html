How To

TUP Cavite Request Credentials Form System

Guidelines:


1. Create an account based on your position
e.g. (Student, Alumnus, Old Student, Faculty)
2. Login using your registered email and password after signing up.
3. From your dashboard, choose the form you you're applying for.
- Graduation Form
- Clearance Form
- Request Form for Credentials
4. Fill up the form and submit.
5. Monitor the updates on the approval of the form applied
using the site.
6. Print the approved form.

For requesting credentials:


* A submitted Form 137-A is required before requesting a certain credentials. (You can also check for your missing requirements on your dashboard).
* For students who successfully applied, wait for the confirmation of your appointment through your registered email.
* Present your printed request form and other requirements provided from the site before arriving at the date and time of your appointment.
* Proceed to the Office of the University Registrar for the next procedures.
* For credentials with payment required, an official receipt should be issued upon claiming.
* The requesting of credentials is done one at a time. No pending request is required before requesting another document.
